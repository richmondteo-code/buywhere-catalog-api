### H2: Major US Grocery Retailers

**Walmart**
The largest retailer in America with "Everyday Low Prices." Supercenters offer one-stop shopping. Walmart+ membership offers fuel discounts and free delivery.

**Target**
Trend-forward retailer with quality groceries and Circle rewards program. Great for one-stop shopping with a more curated selection than Walmart.

**Costco**
Warehouse club requiring membership ($60-120/year). Best for bulk purchases, Kirkland Signature brand, and gas savings. Economics of scale mean 15-30% savings on average.

**Kroger**
The largest traditional supermarket chain in the US. Kroger's digital coupons and card savings stack for significant discounts.

**Whole Foods Market**
Amazon-owned premium grocery chain specializing in organic, natural, and premium foods. Amazon Prime members get exclusive deals.

**Trader Joe's**
Beloved specialty grocery with private-label products and unique selection. No digital coupons — just consistently low prices.

**Safeway, Albertsons, Vons** — Regional chains with loyalty programs.

**Instacart** — Delivery service aggregating multiple stores.
