# BuyWhere vs Pinterest — Product Discovery Compared

Comparing BuyWhere and Pinterest for developers building product discovery experiences.

---

## Overview

**BuyWhere** is a product catalog API and MCP server that provides structured, real-time product pricing and availability data across 500+ retailers. Built for developers who need verified cross-merchant commerce data for AI agents, price comparison tools, and deal aggregators.

**Pinterest** is a visual discovery platform where users browse curated images and videos organized into thematic boards. For commerce, Pinterest offers shoppable pins, product catalogs, and Pinterest Shopping ads — helping brands reach users during the inspiration and discovery phase of shopping.

---

## Key Differences

| Capability | BuyWhere | Pinterest |
|-----------|----------|-----------|
| **Core focus** | Cross-merchant price comparison | Visual discovery and inspiration |
| **Data source** | Direct merchant feeds | Brand-submitted product catalogs |
| **Price comparison** | Cross-merchant, real-time | No |
| **Countries** | US, SG, MY, TH, VN, PH, ID | Global |
| **MCP server** | Yes — @buywhere/mcp-server | No |
| **AI agent native** | Yes | No |
| **Use case** | Price data, deal discovery | Brand awareness, inspiration |

---

## When to Choose BuyWhere

Choose BuyWhere when you need:

- **Cross-merchant price comparison** — compare prices across Amazon, Walmart, Shopee, and 500+ retailers
- **AI agent integration** via MCP for Claude Desktop, Cursor, or custom agents
- **Verified commerce data** from direct merchant feeds — stable, real-time
- **Deal discovery** — find products with active discounts across all retailers
- **Developer-first setup** — API key in minutes, comprehensive documentation

---

## When to Use Pinterest

Use Pinterest when you need:

- **Visual product discovery** — reach users browsing for inspiration (fashion, home decor, DIY)
- **Shoppable pins** — tag products in pins for direct purchase
- **Pinterest Shopping ads** — promote products to users searching for related ideas
- **Brand awareness** — reach users in the early inspiration phase of shopping
- **Catalog integration** — upload your product catalog for automatic pin creation

---

## Summary

BuyWhere and Pinterest serve different product discovery needs. BuyWhere is a **cross-merchant commerce API** — verified real-time pricing across hundreds of retailers for AI agents, price comparison tools, and deal discovery. Pinterest is a **visual discovery platform** — it helps brands reach shoppers during the inspiration phase through shoppable pins and curated content.
