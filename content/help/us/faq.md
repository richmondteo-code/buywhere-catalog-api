# BuyWhere US FAQ — Help Center

## Is BuyWhere free?

Yes, BuyWhere is completely free for US shoppers. Search products, compare prices, and set price alerts at no cost. We monetize through affiliate partnerships—when you purchase through our links, retailers pay us a small commission at no extra cost to you.

## How often do prices update?

Most US retailer prices update every 4–6 hours during business days. High-traffic retailers like Amazon and Walmart may update more frequently—sometimes every 1–2 hours. Each product page shows a "last verified" timestamp.

## Which retailers does BuyWhere cover?

BuyWhere is in US beta with a curated catalog of 2,848 products from Amazon US, Zappos, Nike, and Ulta. We're actively expanding our catalog and retailer partnerships.

## How do price alerts work?

Search for your desired product, click "Alert Me," and enter your target price. When the price drops to your level, we send you an email notification. You can set alerts for specific products or category-wide alerts. There is no limit on how many alerts you can create.

## Can I buy directly through BuyWhere?

No, BuyWhere is a price comparison and discovery platform. Clicking "View Price" takes you directly to the retailer's website where you complete your purchase. This ensures you receive the retailer's full buyer protections, warranties, and return policies.

## How does BuyWhere collect price data?

We use a combination of direct retailer API integrations, approved data feeds, and authorized web scraping to gather US retail pricing. Our systems continuously monitor for price changes and validate data quality through automated checks.

## Does BuyWhere sell my data?

No, BuyWhere does not sell your personal data to third parties. We collect minimal data necessary to operate our service: your email for accounts and alerts, search history for recommendations, and click data for affiliate tracking.

## How does BuyWhere make money?

BuyWhere earns revenue through affiliate marketing partnerships with US retailers. When you click through and purchase, we receive a small commission (typically 1–5% of the sale). This does not affect your price or influence which products we show.

## What if I find an incorrect price?

Use the "Report Incorrect Price" link on the product page. We investigate all reported discrepancies and typically correct errors within 24 hours.

## How do I delete my account?

Log into your account and navigate to Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. We delete your personal data within 30 days per our privacy policy.

## How do I contact support?

For general questions, email support@buywhere.ai. We respond to US shopper inquiries within 1 business day. For API technical issues, developers should check our API documentation first.
