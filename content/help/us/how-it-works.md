# How BuyWhere Works — US Help Center

BuyWhere helps you find the best prices across US retailers by aggregating product data into one searchable catalog. Whether you're comparing prices as a shopper or building an AI shopping agent as a developer, BuyWhere handles the data complexity so you can focus on finding the right product at the right price.

## Finding Products

Enter any product name, brand, or category into the search bar. BuyWhere searches our US beta catalog of 2,848 products across Amazon US, Zappos, Nike, and Ulta. Results return with real-time pricing, ratings, and availability signals.

## Comparing Prices

BuyWhere normalizes pricing data across retailers so you can compare apples to apples. See the same product's price across multiple stores side-by-side, with price-per-unit calculations for varying package sizes. Confidence scores indicate data quality, and price history charts show whether now is a good time to buy.

## Getting Alerts

Set price alerts to monitor items and receive email notifications when prices drop to your target. Search for your desired product, click "Alert Me," and enter your target price. There is no limit on how many alerts you can create.

## Making Purchases

BuyWhere is a price comparison platform. When you find a product you want, clicking "View Price" takes you directly to the retailer's website where you complete your purchase. This ensures you receive the retailer's full buyer protections, warranties, and return policies.

## Price Updates

Most US retailer prices update every 4–6 hours during business days. High-traffic retailers like Amazon and Walmart may update more frequently—sometimes every 1–2 hours. Each product page displays a "last verified" timestamp so you know how recently we checked that price.

## Is BuyWhere Free?

Yes, BuyWhere is completely free for US shoppers. We monetize through affiliate partnerships—when you purchase through our links, retailers pay us a small commission at no extra cost to you. This does not affect which products we show or influence search rankings.

## For Developers

BuyWhere offers a REST API and MCP server integration for developers building AI shopping agents. Query the catalog programmatically, fetch real-time price comparisons, and integrate deal discovery into any AI workflow. The API returns compact, structured JSON optimized for AI agent consumption.
