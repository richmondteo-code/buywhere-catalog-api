## Tips for Getting the Best Price

- **Education pricing:** Apple Education Store offers S$100–200 off MacBooks for students and educators
- **Price-match:** Harvey Norman and Best Denki often match competitor pricing if you ask
- **Timing:** Major sales hit in March (Courts), June–August (Great Singapore Sale), November (11.11)
- **Marketplace vouchers:** Shopee and Lazada rotate vouchers that can save 10–15% on electronics
- **Last gen:** Previous-generation models on Amazon SG can be 20–30% cheaper
