How to apply

Email hello@buywhere.ai with the subject line BuyWhere Developer Beta Application and include:

  • Your name, GitHub, and preferred contact channel
  • What you are building and who it is for
  • Your stack: LLM provider, framework, agent runtime, or MCP setup
  • The query behavior you expect once live
  • Any immediate blocker preventing you from testing quickly

Strong applications usually receive a response within 2 business days. If your use case requires product commitments or roadmap promises, we will route that discussion through the product team before confirming access.
